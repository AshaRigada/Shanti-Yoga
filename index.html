What we do

At Shanti Yoga, our mission is to provide a comprehensive platform for
individuals to incorporate yoga into their daily lives. We offer a wide range of yoga classes,
resources, and information, all taught by experienced and certified instructors.
Our aim is to create a welcoming and inclusive community
where all can experience the transformative power of yoga,
regardless of age, or level of experience.

WHO ARE WE?



Shanti Yoga is a team of passionate and experienced yoga instructors and enthusiasts who are dedicated to sharing the transformative power of yoga with others. We come from diverse backgrounds and have a variety of certifications and specializations, but are united in our love for yoga and our desire to make it accessible to everyone. We believe in creating a welcoming and inclusive community where all feel supported and encouraged in their practice, and are committed to helping our users deepen their understanding and connection to yoga.
